About Us

Welcome to Camp Soteria, a hidden gem nestled in the serene barangay of Salvacion, San Agustin, Surigao del Sur. This idyllic beach resort is the dream realized by a couple who, after years of working abroad, sought solace and purpose in this tranquil paradise. Their hands-on approach to managing Camp Soteria ensures that every guest experiences the love and dedication poured into creating this sanctuary.

The journey to establish Camp Soteria was far from easy. Starting during the pandemic, when travel was limited and the world seemed uncertain, the couple faced numerous challenges. Yet, their unwavering commitment and passion for hospitality saw them through. They transformed this secluded beach into a welcoming haven, where every detail reflects their hard work and vision.

Soteria (from Greek: “deliverance”)

In Hellenistic religions, soteria referred to sacrifices or festivals commemorating or expecting deliverance from a crisis, often involving large-scale commemorative events held at planned intervals. Sixteen such festivals are known, the most famous being at Delphi, celebrating the defeat of the Celts in 279–278 BC.

source: britannica.com

The name 'Soteria,' derived from the Greek word for salvation, embodies the spirit of this place. It is not just a resort but a testament to overcoming obstacles and finding peace. Located in Salvacion, a name that also signifies salvation, the resort stands as a beacon of hope and renewal for all who visit. At Camp Soteria, guests are invited to escape the chaos of everyday life and discover a serene retreat where the soothing sounds of the ocean and the beauty of nature restore their spirit.
